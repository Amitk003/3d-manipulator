How to use

  1. Allow camera access when prompted.
  2. Make sure your palm is visible and facing the camera.
  3. Move palm to move the 3D shape.
  4. Pinch (thumb + index tip) and move fingers closer/farther to scale.
  5. Rotate your palm to rotate the shape.
